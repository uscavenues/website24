ABOUT US

Avenues Consulting Group was formed to give strategists, engineers, and designers a space to collaborate and build projects that positively impact others.

WHAT WE DO

Client Work

The foundation of ACG, client work consists of regular meetings with and without clients to develop strategies and products for them.

Curriculum Night

Especially helpful for those first breaking into consulting, these nights are designed to introduce key concepts to members.

Mini-Hackathons

Our project-builders get the chance to compete in an intra-organizational hackathon to practice their skills and win prizes.

Industry Panels

With many thanks to our mentors, we hold panels and interviews with current and former members of the consulting industry.

Networking Events

From coffee chats with industry experts to networking nights with recruiters, Avenues hosts exclusive events for members only throughout the year.

Workshops

To boost our members' skills and chances for recruitment, we hold workshops for resumes, case interviews, and other relevant concepts.

Case Competitions

For our Strategy Consultants, semesterly case competitions give members a chance to practice planning, pitching, and presenting proposals in 1 hour.

Social Events

From beach hangouts and semesterly retreats, to finals study sessions and team dinners, we make sure to balance the work with the play!
