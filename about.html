ABOUT US

Our Beginnings

Avenues Consulting Group was started in 2023 by a team of students that wanted to address the lack of project-building opportunities available in the consulting space at USC.

Fundraising to support our humble beginnings

Our founding members

Since then, Avenues' membership has grown threefold and acquired a client portfolio of 20+ Fortune 500, non-profit, and startup businesses within 2 years.

Our Mission

Avenues Consulting Group's mission is twofold.

For our clients, Avenues aims to provide pro bono project-building services for organizations ranging from non-profits, to start-ups, to established corporations. We pursue our clients' needs in a multidisciplinary manner, combining our skills in strategy, technology, and design to create complete solutions.

For our consultants, Avenues strives to give strategists, engineers, and designers a space to collaborate and build projects that positively impact others. We are constantly learning new skills and improving the ones we already have. No matter your professional goals, Avenues works to create a space for you.
